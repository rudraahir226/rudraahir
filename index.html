About the Author & CEO

Rudra Ahir

Rudra Ahir is a young author and entrepreneur dedicated to empowering teenagers and young adults through books that inspire growth, discipline, financial wisdom, and self-belief.

At just 16, he founded InkstoneMedia — a publishing brand built from scratch with five books already live on Amazon worldwide. By night, he's a passionate PC gamer who understands exactly what today's generation thinks, feels, and needs to hear.

Through InkstoneMedia, his mission is to create knowledge that makes a real difference in people's lives — by teens, for teens.
