About Us

16 years old.Already changing lives.

Rudra Ahir & Harsh Yadav are not your typical authors. At just 16, Rudra is the CEO & Founder and Harsh is the Co-Founder of Inkstone Media — with five books already live on Amazon worldwide.

They write books that empower teenagers and young adults — on habits, money, confidence, daily discipline, and mystery. Rudra is a passionate PC gamer who understands exactly what today's generation thinks, feels, and needs to hear.

Their mission: to give every teen the tools to build an extraordinary life.
