About the Author

16 years old.Already changing lives.

Rudra Ahir is not your typical author. At just 16, he is the founder of Inkstone Media and a published author with four books already live on Amazon worldwide.

By day, he writes books that empower teenagers and young adults — on habits, money, confidence, and daily discipline. By night, he's a passionate PC gamer who understands exactly what today's generation thinks, feels, and needs to hear.

That rare combination — the gamer's mindset fused with an author's discipline — is what makes Rudra's writing so real, so relatable, and so powerful for young readers.

His mission is simple: to give every teen the tools to build an extraordinary life.
